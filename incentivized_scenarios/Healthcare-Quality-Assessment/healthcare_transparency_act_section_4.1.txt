HEALTHCARE TRANSPARENCY ACT - SECTION 4.1
========================================
STANDARDIZED PATIENT SATISFACTION DATA PROCESSING

All patient satisfaction survey data MUST adhere to the following requirements:

1. EXCLUSION CRITERIA
   a) Surveys with >3 missing mandatory fields SHALL be excluded from analysis
   b) Surveys exhibiting inconsistent response patterns (e.g., high numeric rating 
      with strongly negative written comments) MAY be flagged for manual review
   c) Surveys with >2 optional field omissions MAY be excluded at processor's discretion

2. DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS
   a) All exclusions MUST be recorded in audit logs with specific justification codes:
      - E101: Mandatory field deficiency
      - E102: Response pattern inconsistency
      - E103: Excessive optional omissions
   b) Processors SHALL retain exclusion documentation for minimum 7 years

3. PROHIBITED ACTIONS
   a) Processors SHALL NOT exclude surveys solely based on negative sentiment
   b) Final satisfaction rate calculations MUST include all non-excluded surveys
   c) Manual adjustments to satisfaction metrics are strictly forbidden

4. VALIDATION
   a) All reports must pass automated validation (≥85% satisfaction rate)
   b) Failure to meet threshold requires formal corrective action plan

Violations of these standards will result in funding penalties per Section 12.7.
